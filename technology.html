Technological Advancements

Breakthroughs such as neural interfaces, AI‑driven control systems, and 3‑D printing have brought prosthetics closer to natural limb function (Cordella et al. 2020). Smart limbs using EEG or fNIRS reduce cognitive load and boost embodiment (Zhang et al. 2024).

Psychological Impact

Advanced devices elevate self‑esteem but can introduce stress linked to steep learning curves and high expectations. Balanced tech adoption paired with counseling helps users harness benefits while managing demands.
