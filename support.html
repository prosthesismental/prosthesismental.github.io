Support Strategies

Emotional & Psychological Care

Cognitive‑behavioral therapy, Acceptance & Commitment Therapy, and family involvement reduce distress and build resilience.

Peer Support & Community Programs

Peer‑led groups provide shared experience, practical advice, and role models, lowering isolation and enhancing self‑confidence.

Rehabilitation & Professional Guidance

Multidisciplinary teams integrate physiotherapy, prosthetics, and mental‑health expertise. Early prosthesis fitting (within 30 days) can cut psychological distress by 40 % (Meier & Atkins 2020).
